<h4><strong>Locker Rental Service for Students</strong></h4>
<p>To rent a locker, scan the QR code below or go to <a href="https://go.gov.sg/chrlocker" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">https://go.gov.sg/chrlocker</a>.</p>
<p>Successful allocation of a locker is on a first-come-first-served basis.</p>
<p>Application will close once all lockers are occupied.</p>
